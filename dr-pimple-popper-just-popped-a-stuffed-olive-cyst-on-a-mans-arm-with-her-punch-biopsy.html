Dr. Pimple Popper Just Popped A 'Stuffed Olive' Cyst On A Man's Arm With Her Punch Biopsy Tool

Dr. Pimple Popper captions this Instagram post: "Cheers, #popaholics! Heres a stuffed olive #cyst just in time for Happy Hour! #cheers #drpimplepopper"

Dr. Pimple Popper starts out with a tool she doesn't pop out every day, a punch biopsy tool . She plunges the puncher straight into the cyst on his arm. When she pulls it back out, a column of white gunk comes with it. (FYI: The white gunk in the cyst is actually keratin, which is a major component of skin and hair).

Dr. Pimple Popper drops the tool and switches to fingers. As soon as she starts squeezing, more gunk comes shooting out in a snake-like ribbon. "Oh, you're gonna like this one," she tells her patient as she squeezes. "It's making you laugh."

Once it's all emptied out, she grabs the remnants of the cyst wall with her tweezers. "Oh, it's a very pretty sac actually." Her patient laughed through the appointment and can't help but sneak a peek. When he does, he exclaims "Oh gross."
