TV host introduces Karla Kardashian

During a recent episode of the 'Ellen DeGeneres Show,' the former Oscar host revealed a Kardashian sister who had thus far been unknown. The 57-year-old funny woman appeared before her audience as Karla Kardashian  on Thursday, October 29 - coincidentally just in time for her Halloween special show on Friday, October 30.

The alter-ego was - of course - Ellen dressed up in a gaudy outfit with gigantic fake plastic breasts bulging from her shirt. She donned a jet-black wig, to cover her own cropped blonde locks and completed the transformation with oversized framed glassed. Lashings of make-up embellished her eyes, as the former Oscars host got into character.

Looking like she was enjoying her Halloween costume of choice, Ellen strutted her stuff for the studio audience.
