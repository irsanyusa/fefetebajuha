&quot;I am not trying to be a Tiwa Savage&quot; says actress about her music career

In the interview she reveals that her second album was completed five years ago, but was never released. "I finished my second album since 2010 but it is not yet out contrary to insinuations" she said.

The star actress who has acted in over a hundred movies went on to say that her career should not be compared to that of popular singer Tiwa Savage. "I am not trying to be a Tiwa Savage. My career is like a kind of career" she said.

"I said I would bring out an album every five years. The next step for me is to have my own entertainment place where you can listen to my music, watch my movies and experience everything about Omotola" she further stated.
