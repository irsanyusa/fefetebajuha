Katy Perry's light-blonde eyebrows on the cover of Paper magazine make her look like a different per

On Friday, the magazine published a new cover story and photo shoot starring the pop singer. In the pictures, which were taken by photographer Juno Calypso, Perry has light-blonde eyebrows that are barely visible from certain angles.

Since the singer debuted the bleach-blonde pixie cut she's currently known for in March 2017, her brows have typically been an ash-blonde shade that complements her hair.

On the magazine's cover, Perry's eyebrow transformation is made even more dramatic by a raven-black updo with wipsy strands hanging over her face. The singer is also wearing smokey copper eye-shadow and pale-pink lip gloss the work of makeup artist Sil Bruinsma.

Styled by Italian Vogue editor-at-large Patti Wilson, Perry's avant-garde look is both Elizabethan and futuristic, edgier than her usual colorful, glamorous style .

Perry isn't the only star who has experimented with a new eyebrow look

Kendall Jenner debuted a pair of feathered royal-blue brows on the cover of Allure's March 2019 issue, which was taken by photographer Cass Bird and unveiled earlier this week.

The model's hair and makeup done by hair stylist Tamara McNaughton and makeup artist Romy Soleimani , respectively are kept simple on the cover, allowing her colorful statement brows to shine.

And, of course, who could forget the iconic skinny brows Rihanna rocked on the cover of British Vogue's September 2018 issue, taken by photographer Nick Knight ? Styled by the magazine's editor-in-chief Edward Enninful , Rihanna also wore a Prada dress and gloves, a Savage X Fenty lace bodysuit, and Fenty Beauty makeup.

The singer's unforgettable makeup look was done by makeup artist and creative director Isamaya Ffrench .
