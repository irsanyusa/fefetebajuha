A Woman, 58, Harshly Criticized For Not Acting Age-Appropriate, Shatters Internet With Her Daring Ph

Paulina Porizkova, a former supermodel, faced ageist comments after sharing bikini photos on social media. Some even went so far as to call her a “desperate grandma.” In response, she delivered an empowering message about body positivity and self-love.

She began her career at a young age.

ASSOCIATED PRESS / East News

Paulina Porizkova, born in 1965, started her modeling journey at just 15 years old. She’s been featured on the covers of many prestigious magazines. Beyond modeling, she’s appeared in various films, TV shows, and music videos. Lately, she’s gained recognition for her honest and thought-provoking writing, covering subjects like beauty standards, grief, and loss.

Paulina Porizkova is a fierce advocate against ageism, both in society and her personal life.

At 57 years old, Paulina Porizkova has challenged societal norms by proudly modeling and sharing bikini photos on social media. She fearlessly criticizes ageist attitudes, emphasizing that age should not limit anyone from expressing themselves.

Despite facing negative comments, she remains steadfast, fighting against stereotypes like being called a “desperate grandma” or being told she’s “too old to be doing this.”

During her 28-year marriage to Ric Ocasek, the legendary frontman of The Cars, Paulina Porizkova, openly acknowledged that their relationship had its ups and downs. In an interview, she revealed that they had experienced periods of estrangement before his passing in 2019. She spoke honestly about their challenges, sharing that they gradually drifted apart, communicated less, and he stopped showing desire for her.

Porizkova also revealed her doubts about aging, expressing, “Since I was hitting my late 40s and early 50s, I think I figured, maybe he doesn’t fancy me because I’m getting older.” Despite these challenges, she emphasized the love and connection they shared and has continued to honor Ocasek’s memory since his passing.

Porizkova champions celebrating aging for women.

Now, Paulina Porizkova passionately advocates celebrating aging and fully embracing the natural aging process. She firmly stands by her decision not to undergo plastic surgery or cosmetic enhancements, expressing, “My desire is to be an unaltered older woman.”

Moreover, Porizkova appreciates the physical signs of aging, viewing them as a beautiful record of the experiences and stories that have shaped her life. At 57, she sees her face as a captivating novel that tells the rich history of her journey.

Beyond her personal choices, Porizkova strongly advocates for a shift in society’s attitudes toward aging. She highlights the significance of women embracing their age and proudly showcasing their achievements. She firmly believes in making aging cool and encourages more women to come forward and proudly declare, “Look at what I’m doing at my age!”

Through her own example and advocacy work, Porizkova has become a powerful voice in the movement to redefine aging and promote self-love at any age.
