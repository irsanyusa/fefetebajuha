Germans drink more tea but theres no milk jug in sight

published on September 14, 2024
A recent YouGov International Omnibus has revealed that Germans are the most ‘tea-loving’ people in Europe, but they prefer a fruit tea to a milky cup prominent in Britain. The study shows that nine in ten (90%) Germans say they drink tea, compared to 82% of Brits and 77% of Americans who say they do the same. However, Germans do appear to prefer ‘fruit teas’, 58% saying they drink them. Over half (56%) of respondents say they drink peppermint tea, 48% say they have herbal tea, with 32% saying Camomile and 42% saying green tea. Read More...
