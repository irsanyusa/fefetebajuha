Lizzo recently took to Instagram to show herself trying on some new pieces from her Yitty clothing line. Some fans took note of what appears to be the singer’s weight loss. Commenters have made similar observations on her other recent posts.

Earlier this week, Lizzo posted a video in which she tries on some new sports bras and leggings from her clothing line, Yitty. As she said at the start of the clip, “New year, new me.” She admitted that the clothes were hard to get into, adding that it “snatches and lifts.”

While some commented on the clothes, others were more focused on whether Lizzo has lost weight. One fan wrote, “We see u shedding pounds love. Even tho u were beautiful before.” The comment has over 3,000 likes as of this writing.

Fans made similar comments on a later post by the singer, as she posed in a revealing dress. As one user wrote, “Somebody is slimming and trimming. I see ya!!” Another commented, “I knew eventually she would jump on weight loss wagon lol.”

The Singer’s Dog Recently Passed Away

Last month, Lizzo shared that her dog, Pooka, had passed away on Christmas Eve. Pooka was 18 years old when she passed away. A few days later, Lizzo shared a few throwback photos of the dog, writing, “I have cried every day. I miss you Pooka. So so much. Thank you for almost 20 years of love.”

Meanwhile, Lizzo has been dealing with a legal controversy, after being sued by her former employees over alleged sexual harassment. The singer responded to the allegations last August, calling them “outrageous.”

“Sometimes I have to make hard decisions but it’s never my intention to make anyone feel uncomfortable or like they aren’t valued as an important part of the team,” Lizzo wrote in her statement.
