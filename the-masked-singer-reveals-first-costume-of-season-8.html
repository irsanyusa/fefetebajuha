The Masked Singer‘s eighth season is right around the corner and fans cannot contain their excitement. After months of waiting, the show has finally premiered their first costume of the season. Dragon Bride is totally ready to walk down The Masked Singer aisle.

Meet Bride, The Masked Singer Season 8’s First Costume

The Masked Singer returns next month with a brand new list of larger than life celebrities. Since the show’s premiere is only weeks away, the network has revealed the first costume of the season. Meet Dragon Bride, a horrifyingly beautiful bridezilla, Bride.

This costume features a pink hardshell dinosaur costume with bright yellow eyes. Accessories on this costume feature a vail, wedding dress, pointy nails, and white sparkly shoes. Whoever Bride is, must have a great sense of style. Bride was created by costume designer Tim Chappel, who is known for working on The Masked Singer Australia. Fans are excited to hear about Chappel’s involvement in the USA version of the series.

FOX has yet to reveal any other costumes at this time. The Masked Singer returns to the network on Wednesday, September 21 at 8 p.m. ET. As always the panel is set to feature Jenny McCarthy, Nicole Scherzinger, Robin Thicke, and Ken Jeong. The show is hosted by Nick Cannon once again. The network recently revealed that this season will feature performances by both Thicke and Scherzinger.

Fans believe that popular football player, Tom Brady will appear on this season of the series. For those who may not have heard, a rumor recently started due to Brady’s absence from the Buccaneers training camp. Although the football quarterback has denied the rumors on Twitter, many still believe he’ll appear on the series.

This isn’t the first time celebrity identities have been revealed ahead of the season. Last season, Rudy Giuliani’s unmasking leaked and cause controversy for the celebrity guessing game. Since his involvement made national headlines, the show teased his appearance for several weeks before his reveal.
