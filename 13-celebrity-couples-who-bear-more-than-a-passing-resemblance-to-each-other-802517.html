13 Celebrity Couples Who Bear More Than a Passing Resemblance to Each Other

According to a study, couples tend to begin to look alike over time, even if they didn’t have facial similarities at the beginning of their relationship. Celebrity couples are no exception to that, and by looking at their joint pictures and seeing the resemblance, we might even think they are related to each other.

We at Bright Side were surprised at how some celebrity couples look so alike. And once we saw the resemblance, we couldn’t unsee it.

1. Courteney Cox and Johnny McDaid

2. Vincent Kartheiser and Alexis Bledel

3. Rooney Mara and Joaquin Phoenix

4. Leighton Meester and Adam Brody

5. Jason Momoa and Lisa Bonet

6. Sophie Hunter and Benedict Cumberbatch

7. Alexi Ashe and Seth Meyers

8. Nate Berkus and Jeremiah Brent

9. Rande Gerber and Cindy Crawford

10. Eddie Cibrian and LeAnn Rimes

11. Jessica Alba and Cash Warren

12. Justin Bieber and Hailey Bieber

13. Robbie Williams and Ayda Field
