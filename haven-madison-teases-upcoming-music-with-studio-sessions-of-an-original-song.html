Haven Madison Teases Upcoming Music With Studio Sessions Of An Original Song

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

ABC/Eric McCandless

Reading Time: 2 minutes

American Idol Season 21 contestant Haven Madison has been creating buzz among her fans, while hinting about upcoming releases. Through a series of intriguing Instagram posts, Madison has provided glimpses into her recording process and also shared a heartfelt original song. As anticipation builds, fans eagerly await the release of her upcoming music.

Haven Madison Teases Upcoming Music

Madison’s journey into the music world continues to evolve as she recently shared a behind-the-scenes glimpse into her time in the recording studio. A series of Instagram posts immediately sparked excitement among her followers, signaling that something special was in the works.

In one of her posts, Madison delighted fans by revealing that she had finished tracking vocals for her upcoming project. This update hinted at the progress she had made and hinted that her music was one step closer to reaching the eager ears of her supporters.

Building up the anticipation even further, Madison treated her followers to a captivating video of her performing an original song. In the clip, she shared that she had just returned from a young life camp, which then inspired her to write a heartfelt track about her experiences and emotions.

In the same Instagram post, Madison also actively engaged her followers by requesting their input for song title ideas. By encouraging fans to contribute their suggestions, she not only sparked excitement but also involved her audience in the creative process.

She’s Set To Perform With Season Runner-Up Megan Danielle at a Summer Bash

In addition to her exciting upcoming music, Madison has made another thrilling announcement that is sure to delight her fans. She will be joining forces with fellow American Idol Season 21 competitor and runner-up, Megan Danielle, for a special performance at the Franklin Summer Bash.

Haven Madison and Megan Danielle will hold the highly-anticipated concert event on Saturday, August 5, 2023, from 7:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. CDT at Liberty Hall in The Factory at Franklin, 37064. The schedule for the evening is as follows: VIP doors will open at 4:00 p.m., general admission doors at 6:30 p.m., and the show will kick off at 7:00 p.m. It’s best to purchase tickets in advance to secure your spot. General admission tickets are priced at $25 ($35 if purchased on the day of the show, subject to availability).

This event is open to all ages, promising an enjoyable experience for fans of all generations. Please note that all sales are final, so be sure to grab your tickets early to avoid missing out on this unforgettable night of music.
