Subscription boxes win over 14% of Brits

published on August 05, 2024
Subscription box fans cite convenience as the most appealing aspect, while cost puts off many other consumersAbout one in seven people (14%) have bought from a subscription box company in the last twelve months, with snacks and drinks (33%), meal-in-a-box ingredient services (22%) and make-up and beauty products (20%) being the most popular types. About two in five subscription box users (37%) cite convenience as a reason for signing up. A third (33%) say they want to treat themselves, while about three in ten (31%) do it to try new products.
