JoJo Siwa's Girl Group XOMG POP is Launching a New TV Show

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by: Greg Gayne/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

XOMG POP, The teen pop group created by JoJo and Jessalynn Siwa is launching their new TV show. Titled The XOMG POP Show, the holiday-themed show sees the favorite girls celebrating the Christmas tradition in their own special way.

XOMG POP Announced Their New Show

XOMG POP is lighting up the holidays with their brand new upcoming show The XOMG POP Show. The upcoming show about the girl group will be available to stream on their YouTube channel.

The group posted the first trailer of the show on their official Instagram page. Not much about the synopsis of the show has been revealed, except that the show takes place at Christmas time. All we know is that the group will be having a Christmas party, hosted by member Brooklynn Pitts.

The show will have its own story about how the party will have unexpected surprises like a gingerbread house being way too small. An exciting holiday performance from XOMG POP will be shown on the show as well, if they can find Tinie T on time.

The Show will air on December 9, 2022, and will mark as another highly anticipated project from the girl group. Apart from releasing new music, XOMG POP is also selling multiple products and merchandise on their official website.

Fans are Excited For the Upcoming Show

Fans of the girl group are excited for the announcement of their upcoming show, giving their support for XOMG POP. Most fans cannot wait until December 9 for the show, in turn they have expressed their patience through heartfelt comments.

“This is going to be so exciting can’t wait,” an Instagram user commented.

“Literally on the edge of our seats! Why is it taking so long?” Another user added.

While XOMG POP fans await the highly anticipated TV show, in the meantime, they will be able to watch the group perform at the Children’s and Family Emmy Awards. JoJo is announced as the host of the event along with Jack McBrayer, and there’s no way the girl group would let the chance to perform pass by.
