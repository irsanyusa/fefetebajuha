Tenzin Gyatso (14th Dalai Lama)

Full Name: Lhamo Dondrub
Profession: 14th Dalai Lama

Nationality: Tibetan

Biography: Spiritual leader of Tibetans.

During the 1959 Tibetan uprising, the Dalai Lama fled Tibet for India, where he currently lives as a refugee.

He has since traveled the world, advocating for the welfare of Tibetans and teaching Tibetan Buddhism.

He received the Nobel Peace Prize in 1989.

Born: July 6, 1935
Birthplace: Taktser, Qinghai, China
Age: 89 years old

Generation: Silent Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Pig
Star Sign: Cancer
