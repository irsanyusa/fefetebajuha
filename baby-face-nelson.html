Baby Face Nelson (Bank Robber and Gangster)

Full Name: Lester Joseph Gillis
Profession: Bank Robber and Gangster

Biography: Lester Joseph Gillis, better known as Baby Face Nelson, was an American bank robber known for his partnership with John Dillinger. Nelson helped Dillinger to escape, famously using a wooden pistol, from a prison in Crown Point, Indiana.

Nelson had been an established criminal in his own right prior to meeting Dillinger, starting with robberies at an early age and ultimately going on to lead his own gang. This group is believed to have aided in Dillinger's escape, after which the two groups entered into a partnership.

Known as a particular violent bank robber, Nelson killed more FBI agents in his career than any other criminal. They finally had their revenge when he was killed by FBI agents at the Battle of Barrington which was fought in Chicago, Illinois.

Born: December 6, 1908
Birthplace: Chicago, Illinois, USA

Generation: Greatest Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Monkey
Star Sign: Sagittarius

Died: November 27, 1934 (aged 25)
Cause of Death: Shot by FBI agents in a shootout in Barrington, Illinois known as the Battle of Barrington
